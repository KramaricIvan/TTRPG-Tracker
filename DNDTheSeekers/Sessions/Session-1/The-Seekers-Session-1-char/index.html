      • AAron and Potjeh get fed up again and just leave the group and start walking towards the Dyr Mountains
      • Rungni follows them and revelas he is going for the Dyr mountains in search of dwarven settlements, to which Potjeh opens up about his search for truth.
      • þrúđr and Rungni question AAron about why is he so tied to Potjeh and why is he always looking down at the floor

      • Taboo’s tunes into the familar’s senses, and scouts the camp they found on the road. Finding out the camp was burned and full of blood
      • The Seekers together roll lower investigation than AAron alone, which gets him some coins, an expensive ring, tattered bloody notebook
      • AAron reads the note written in infernal aloud
      • In the forest Rungni manages to get a glimpse of a random sprite buzzing around them
      • The Seekers are ready to set camp, Potjeh and AAron march forward to check for danger, þrúđr and Rungni check the back while Khashana and Taboo collect twigs and clean up the sleeping spots
      • During first sleep watch AAron is sitting near the campfire and working on his masks, painting a facemask and carving a branch to look like Satyr horns while Potjeh is trying to discern different flowers in the darkness
      • AAron hears a random noise behind himself and casts a shower of sparks. Revealing a small frightened purple-pink sprite. He tries to talk to it in Sylvan, asking for it’s intentions and why it is there
      • Potjeh offers some fruti to the sprite, while AAron backs away to clear the space
      • The sprite approaches, revealing it’s name is Thistle and that it feels like !!! AAron is a good man !!! as well as saying it loves centaurs and Potjeh
      • Potjeh is very confused about what centaurs are which confuses Thistle. Potjeh is very convinced he is just a weirdly built human and asks AAron to write it all down, it might be a key to findout out The Truth
      • AAron, Potjeh and Thistle chat into the night, Thistle and AAron bond over a fire while carving the satyr-like horn
      • Potjeh wakes up Taboo and Khashana for their watch, but AAron tells them to be quiet and introduces them to Thistle
      • Thistle and AAron become BFFs
